Featured Work

ThreatScan

Dark web monitoring for schools at $0.25/user
The Problem

Education data breaches are a growing crisis, and existing monitoring tools cost hundreds. Smaller districts can't afford them.

What We Built

A product that scans district domains against dark web breaches, matches compromised credentials, and lets admins force password resets.

Validation

At a customer conference, a tenant admin told us this would be transformative for small districts. At $0.25/user, security becomes accessible to everyone.

Scale

22M+ users across 3,000+ school systems in 42 countries.

AppTrack

The Problem
